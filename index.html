about us

Discharge from the Emergency Department represents a complex transition from having constant support from nurses and physicians to being discharged home to self and family care. The stress and anxiety involved with being discharged home intensifies patient’s needs for information, education and reassurance. The ED Video Discharge Education system allows you to view a designated video on your discharge diagnosis and replay the video as often as needed to review the contents and learn at your own pace. Our goal is to increase patient knowledge of their discharge instructions and increase patient satisfaction of their discharge process.

Providing Important Information

Providing important information on discharge instructions that patients and family are able to access anytime

B

Increase Patient's Knowledge

Increase patient’s knowledge of their discharge instructions to decrease the risk of adverse events, repeat ED visits and readmission after discharge.

Increase Patient's Satisfaction

Increase patient satisfaction of the ED discharge process and overall care
